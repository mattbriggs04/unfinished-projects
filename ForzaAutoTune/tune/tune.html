Make your Tune

Follow the directions below. Make sure you are in the tune menu of the game.

Step 1: Pick an end focus for your tune

Step 2: Find your front weight %

You can find your front weight % by going to the festival, going to 'Upgrades & Tuning' then 'Custom Upgrade.'
From there, click on any upgrade, and then on your controller press up on the d-pad until a screen similar to the image on the right appears

Step 3: Enter your min-max values

There are three different min-max values that you'll have to manually input to ensure your results are perfect: Damping, Spring, and Antiroll Bars. In the image below, the min is 3 and max 20.
